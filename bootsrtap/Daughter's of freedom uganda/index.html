About us

WHO WE ARE



Daughters of Freedom Uganda (DOFU) formerly (Destiny Women Uganda) is founded by Late Mrs. Miriam Nabushawo Enyaga and Mr. Enyaga Fulukas Boroa asCommunity Based Organization (CBO) in 2010 at Namusera in Wakiso Town Council and later legally registered as Daughters of Freedom Uganda a Non-GovernmentalOrganization (NGO) in 2020 with offices at Mpererwe Kawempe Division, Kampala

Vision



Our Vision is a community free of commercial sex trafficking and exploitation.

MISSION



Our mission is to restore hope, identity, dignity, and destiny of victims and survivors of commercial sex exploitation and trafficking by Preventing and Rescuing, Rehabilitating,Reuniting and Reintegrating victims back to the community
